Vibrant, playful, and organized: redesigning Vremi's e-commerce experience

Vremi is a home appliances brand known for its colorful and playful products. While this personality was its strength, the old website felt overwhelming and chaotic due to inconsistent use of colors and poorly structured content. The redesign aimed to bring order and clarity without losing the brand's vibrant identity.

Goals

Improve clarity and readability of the website.

Highlight seasonal promotions in a way that is visible but not intrusive.

Group products into intuitive categories.

Create a seamless user journey to increase conversion rates.

Design Process

The main audience consists of young homeowners and renters (25-45) looking for functional yet stylish small appliances. They value affordability, playful aesthetics, and convenience in online shopping.
