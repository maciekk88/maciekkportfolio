ClientHealing Solutions

Redesigning an online store to elevate the brand and simplify the shopping experience.

Healing Solutions is a brand specializing in essential oils. Their previous store felt outdated, chaotic, and difficult to navigate. The goal was to create a modern, intuitive e-commerce platform that positions the oils as refined wellness products.

Project Goals

Upgrade outdated and chaotic design into a refined, wellness-inspired look.

Streamline the shopping flow and checkout.

Ensure responsive design with a mobile-friendly shopping experience.

Design Process

We started with competitor analysis to understand best practices in the wellness & aromatherapy industry. Then we created a style guide to unify visuals and ensure consistency. Wireframes followed, focusing on accessibility of functions and clarity of navigation. Finally, we designed the UX & UI in line with the brand's refined positioning.

Research & AnalysisWireframesStyle GuideUX & UI Design

Final Design

The redesigned store emphasizes clarity, wellness-inspired aesthetics, and a refined brand image. Essential oils are showcased to highlight their natural origins and quality, while the checkout flow is intuitive and frictionless.

The design was built full responsive, with mobile views tailored to make browsing and purchasing essential oils simple, fast, and enjoyable across all devices.

Summary & Results

Healing Solutions' redesigned online store successfully improved usability, reinforced the brand's refined perception, and made essential oils more accessible to the target audience.
