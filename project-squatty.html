ClientSquatty Potty

Designing an online store for a brand that helps people sit better, feel better.

Squatty Potty is a global brand producing toilet stools that improve posture during bowel movements for better health. Their product range spans from standard models, through fun, kid-friendly designs, to premium editions for style-conscious customers. The challenge was to create an e-commerce experience that not only sells but also educates users on the benefits of proper bathroom posture.

Context & Goals

The goal was to design a modern e-commerce website for Squatty Potty that not only sells but also educates users about proper bathroom posture.

Key Points

Combining storytelling with conversion.

Educational content based on real data.

Fully responsive design for all age groups.

Design Process

We started with market research and competitor analysis to find a unique communication angle. Using real data and user feedback, we shaped a clear message and user-friendly structure.
